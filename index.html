About Us

Pet Relocation is a trusted pet transport and relocation company dedicated to making pet moving services seamless and stress-free. With years of experience in the pet industry, we specialize in both international and domestic pet shifting, ensuring your beloved companions travel safely from one corner of the globe to another. Our team understands that pets are family, not cargo, and we treat every relocation with the utmost care and compassion.

We handle all aspects of pet relocation, including comprehensive safety measures, full documentation support, quarantine assistance, custom travel crates, veterinary health checks, and ongoing comfort during transit. Whether it's dog shifting or cat relocation, our experts provide personalized attention to minimize stress and ensure a smooth journey.

At Pet Relocation, trust and transparency are at our core. We partner with airlines, vets, and customs officials to navigate pet customs clearance and international pet travel regulations. Choose us for reliable pet cargo services that prioritize your pet's well-being above all.

Our Services

International Pet Relocation

We specialize in global pet transport, handling dog shifting and cat relocation across borders. Our team manages all logistics for safe, compliant international pet travel, ensuring your pet arrives healthy and happy.

Domestic Pet Transport

For within-country moves, our domestic pet transport services offer reliable pet moving services. We provide door-to-door pickup and delivery, with expert handlers ensuring comfort during short or long journeys.

Pet Travel Documentation & Licensing

Navigate pet documentation effortlessly with our support for licenses, permits, and forms. We assist with all required paperwork for pet customs clearance, making international pet travel stress-free.

Veterinary Health Certificates

Obtain vet-certified health certificates essential for pet relocation. Our partnerships with licensed veterinarians ensure your pet meets all health standards for travel, including vaccinations and exams.

IATA Approved Travel Crates

We supply custom, IATA-approved travel crates designed for pet safety and comfort. These crates meet airline standards for pet cargo, reducing stress during flights and transports.

Airport Pickup & Drop

Enjoy hassle-free airport services with our pickup and drop-off options. We handle pet transport from home to airport and back, coordinating with airlines for smooth pet relocation.

Quarantine & Customs Clearance

Expert assistance with quarantine requirements and pet customs clearance. We guide you through international regulations, ensuring compliance and timely reunions with your pets.

Live Pet Tracking & Support

Monitor your pet's journey with live tracking and 24/7 support. Our GPS-enabled systems provide real-time updates, giving pet parents peace of mind during pet transport.

Pet Boarding (if required during transit)

Temporary pet boarding services for layovers or delays. We offer safe, comfortable facilities with vet checks, ensuring your pet's well-being while awaiting the next leg of travel.
